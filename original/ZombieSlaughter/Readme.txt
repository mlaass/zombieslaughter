About
/+----------
Tthis Game was made during the Classic Ludumdare.com
48 hours Game-Programming competition.
I hop you have fun with this game although its far from perfect.

Controls
/+------------


[Left]/[Right], [A]/[D] Keys :  Steer
[Up]/[w]/[Space] Keys :         Jump
[MOUSE_B]/[CTRL] Keys :         Shoot


	Other Keys:
	/+----------------
	M Key :		Mute music
	P Key :		Play music



Help
/+-----------

In The Menu navigate with your UP and DOWN cursor 
buttons. To select a menu item press ENTER.

folling commandlines argumentare possible:
-windowed
-debug
-color <colordepth>
